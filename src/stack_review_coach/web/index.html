System Stack Review and Coach

Learn what is installed, what it does, and how your tools fit together.

A guided, local-first stack tour for newer developers. The app runs local probe agents on your machine, turns their findings into plain-language coaching, and shows how common tools connect.

Nothing leaves your machine. Reviews happen through local command probes.
